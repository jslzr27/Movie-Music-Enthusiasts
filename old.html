      Welcome to Movie Music Enthusiasts!


      This site is designed for movie and music lovers a-like, but more-so for those who love the music in movies.
      Music has the ability to elevate a scene. In one moment it has your heart pounding as the beat amplifies the explosions of an action sequence, and in another it's sweet melody breaks you down into a sobbing mess.
      No matter what your reason for visiting, we hope you leave having discovered more music and movies to enjoy.
      To get started, choose the movie category to receive music suggestions based on the music in the movies you love, or a music category to receive suggestions on movies you may enjoy for their music content.

      Happy Discovering!
